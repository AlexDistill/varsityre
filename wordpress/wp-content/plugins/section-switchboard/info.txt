== Description ==

<h3>Time-saved in a box.</h3>

<p>For developers &amp; anyone else who doesn't want to lose years of their life activating &amp; deactivating sections individually - this plugin is for you! &lt;3</p>

<h3>New With v1.1 - Optimize Your Site In a Flash!</h3>
<p>With version 1.1, you can SEE not only which sections are currently activated or deactivated, but which sections are active <em>but not currently being used! (inactive)</em>.</p>
<p>With the new <strong>Toggle Inactive</strong> button, you can check/uncheck all of these at once.</p>

<h3>Toggles For Every Occassion</h3>
<p>Check them all on, all off, or toggle only activated, inactive or deactivated sections - Section Switchboard helps you make the change in as few clicks as possible.</p>

<h3>How To Use</h3>
<ol>
	<li>Install through the Store</li>
	<li>Navigate to the new <em>Section Switchboard</em> menu page under the main PageLines menu</li>
	<li>Check/Uncheck individually &amp;/or use the various select toggle buttons at the top of each section type</li>
	<li>Click <em>Apply Changes</em></li>
	<li><strong>DONE.</strong> (You will even see a message about how many sections were activated &amp; deactivated!)</li>
</ol>

<p>Your brain cells will thank you.</p>
