== Description ==

Wicker; a full-width slider with optional Wordpress menu and overlay patterns.

This section looks great when used in the header area, as it has optional support for Wordpress menus. It will stretch completely from left to right 100%, with optional Hero support and multiple content sources.

<h3>Features</h3>
<strong>Full Width</strong> stretches from left to right 100% and is fully responsive.

<strong>Optional Menu Control</strong> optionally show a one-level Wordpress menu, with control for alignment.

<strong>Optional Pattern Overlay</strong> instantly enhance any of your beautiful images with a super cool pattern overlay.

<strong>Slide Options</strong> this slider is the lightest and most simple slider available. Specify up to 10 slides, set the slides to rotate automatically, or randomize them.

<strong>Hero Support</strong> easily show a Hero Unit (text/image/button), and even specify whether it should use PageLines options for slides, or Wordpress Posts.
